Why Backlinks Matter for SEO

Google's Original PageRank Algorithm

Google's founders Larry Page and Sergey Brin revolutionized search by using links as votes of confidence. The basic premise remains relevant today:

  • Each link to your page is like a vote
  • Links from authoritative sites count more
  • Relevant links carry more weight
  • Link equity flows through your site via internal links

Rankings

Backlinks are among Google's top 3 ranking factors

Discovery

Links help Google discover and index your pages

Referral Traffic

Quality links send targeted visitors to your site

Understanding Link Quality

Not all links are created equal. Understanding quality metrics helps prioritize your link building efforts:

Domain Authority (DA)

A score (1-100) predicting a website's ranking potential. Higher DA sites pass more link equity.

Target range: DA 40+ for valuable links, but relevance matters more than DA alone

Relevance

Links from sites in your niche or industry carry more weight than random links.

Best practice: A DA 30 link from a relevant site beats a DA 70 link from an unrelated site

Placement

Where the link appears on the page affects its value:

  • Best: Within the main content body
  • Good: In sidebar widgets or author bios
  • Worst: Footer links or sitewide links

Dofollow vs Nofollow

The rel attribute tells Google how to treat the link:

<a href="...">

Dofollow (default): Passes link equity

<a href="..." rel="nofollow">

Nofollow: Doesn't pass equity (still valuable for traffic)

Guest Blogging Strategy

Guest posting remains one of the most effective white-hat link building strategies when done correctly.

Step-by-Step Process

  1. 1
    Find Target Sites

    Use Google search operators: "your keyword" + "write for us" OR "guest post" OR "contributor guidelines"

  2. 2
    Qualify Sites

    Check DA, traffic, audience relevance, and existing content quality

  3. 3
    Study Their Content

    Read 5-10 posts to understand tone, style, and topics

  4. 4
    Pitch Unique Topics

    Propose original angles, not rehashed content they've already published

  5. 5
    Deliver Exceptional Content

    Exceed their word count and quality expectations

Guest Posting Best Practices

  • Only write for sites with real audiences (not just link farms)
  • Include 1-2 contextual links max (don't overdo it)
  • Link to your best relevant content, not just homepage
  • Engage with comments after publication
  • Promote your guest post on social media

Broken Link Building

Find broken links on relevant sites and offer your content as a replacement. It's a win-win for everyone.

How It Works

Step 1: Find resource pages in your niche

Search: "your keyword" + "resources" + inurl:links

Step 2: Use tools to find broken links

Check My Links extension, Ahrefs, or Screaming Frog

Step 3: Create replacement content

Make sure your content covers the same topic better

Step 4: Reach out to the site owner

Politely point out the broken link and suggest your resource

The Skyscraper Technique

Popularized by Brian Dean, this technique involves finding popular content and making it better.

The 3-Step Process

1
Find Link-Worthy Content

Search for content with lots of backlinks in your niche using Ahrefs or SEMrush

2
Create Something Better

Make it longer, more updated, better designed, with more examples and data

3
Reach Out to Linkers

Contact everyone who linked to the original content and suggest yours

Ways to Make Content "Better"

  • Add 50%+ more content depth
  • Update outdated information
  • Include original data, surveys, or case studies
  • Add better visuals (charts, infographics, videos)
  • Improve design and readability
  • Add practical examples and templates

Digital PR Strategies

Earning links through newsworthy content and journalist outreach.

Newsjacking

Piggyback on trending news stories with your expert commentary or data.

Example: When AI trends spike, publish "How AI is Transforming [Your Industry]"

Original Studies

Publish surveys, experiments, or research that journalists will cite.

Example: "We surveyed 1,000 marketers about their SEO budget"

Online Calculators

Create free tools that provide value and earn natural links.

Example: ROI calculator, cost comparison tool, savings estimator

Awards & Lists

Create annual awards or lists that recipients will link to.

Example: "Top 100 [Industry] Blogs to Follow in 2024"

Journalist Outreach Platforms

Connect with journalists looking for expert sources:

  • Help a Reporter Out (HARO) - Free queries from journalists 3x/day
  • Qwoted - Connect with journalists seeking expert quotes
  • ResponseSource - UK-based journalist requests
  • TERAKEET - Media opportunities for brands

Resource Page Link Building

Many websites maintain resource pages with curated links. Getting listed is a sustainable link building tactic.

Google Search Operators

Use these operators to find resource pages in your niche:

"your keyword" + "resources" + inurl:resources.html
"your keyword" + "useful links" + inurl:links
"your keyword" + "recommended sites" + "add url"

Pro Tips for Resource Pages

  • Look for recently updated pages (check the footer)
  • Check if the page has any broken links (your opportunity)
  • Verify the page actually gets traffic (use SimilarWeb)
  • Personalize your outreach referencing specific resources on the page

Email Outreach Templates

Effective outreach is personalized, concise, and provides value upfront.

Anchor Text Optimization

The clickable text in a link (anchor text) helps search engines understand your page's topic.

Exact Match

"blue running shoes"

Most powerful but risky if overused

Partial Match

"best shoes for running"

Good balance of relevance and safety

Branded

"Nike official site"

Natural and safe

Generic

"click here" / "learn more"

Natural but less descriptive

Anchor Text Best Practices

  • Keep it natural and diverse (avoid repetition)
  • Match the linked page's content
  • For guest posts, use branded or URL anchors
  • Avoid over-optimization (too many exact-match links looks spammy)

Link Reclamation

Recover lost link value by fixing issues that caused you to lose backlinks.

404 Error Links

Find sites linking to your 404 pages and ask them to update to the correct URL.

Tool: Google Search Console > Coverage > Excluded > 404

Redirect Issues

Check if redirects are working properly and not losing link equity.

Tool: Ahrefs > Backlinks > "Lost" filter

Incorrect Mentions

Find mentions of your brand without links and request one.

Tool: Ahrefs Content Explorer > Mention alerts

Broken Backlinks

When a linking site breaks, find the new URL or ask for removal.

Action: Monitor backlink profile regularly

Unlinked Brand Mentions

Find when people mention your brand without linking and politely request a link.

How to Find Unlinked Mentions

  • Google Alerts: Set up alerts for your brand name, products, and key people
  • Ahrefs Mentions: Content Explorer > "Mentions" tab
  • BuzzSumo: Monitor brand mentions across the web
  • Mention.net: Real-time mention tracking

Outreach Template

I noticed the mention didn't include a link to our site. Would it be possible to add one? Here's our URL for reference: [your URL]


Either way, thanks for the shout-out!


Best,
[Your Name]

Common Link Building Mistakes to Avoid

Buying Links

Violates Google's guidelines and can result in manual penalties. Always earn links through value, not payment.

PBNs (Private Blog Networks)

Networks of sites created solely for link building. Google has gotten very good at detecting these.

Link Exchanges/Reciprocal Links

Excessive link trading ("I'll link to you if you link to me") can be seen as manipulative.

Article Directories

Low-quality article submission sites are devalued by Google and can hurt your SEO.

Comment Spam

Posting comments with links solely for backlinks. These are almost always nofollow anyway.

Forum Spam

Dropping links in forums without adding value. Most forums use nofollow anyway.

Ignoring Relevance

Getting links from unrelated sites. Context matters more than ever.

Over-Optimized Anchor Text

Too many exact-match keywords in anchor text looks unnatural and can trigger penalties.
